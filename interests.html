A large portion of who I am comes down to what I do and that trickles down to the activities I indulge in as pastimes. These are just amongst the few things I enjoy taking part in:

  1. Coding

    2. Needless to say, coding is one of my major enjoyable pastimes. It was one of those activities that was a passion of mine that met work to become my greatest hobby. It manages to equally combine a variety of attributes I look for such as learning, innovation, challenge and creation.

  2. Exercising

    3. To begin with, I absolutely relish exercising, specifically in the gym. Just the sight of a pair of 5 kg dumbbells is enough to get me going. I like challenging my body to various degrees such as strength and endurance; something enabled through weight training.

  3. Watching social commentaries on contemporary socio-political issues

    4. I like gathering various schools of thoughts so as to challenge my own and observe trends in society. Each scintillating conversation brings up a new way that I can challenge my thought as well as helps in interacting and networking with others.

  4. Sewing

    5. I also enjoy sewing which I picked up last year (2020). There’s nothing better than curating a perfectly custom-fitted outfit for yourself, I recently discovered. It utilizes the same attributes as coding, which are creativity, innovation, skill and creation. This makes it all the more enjoyable.

  5. Cooking

    6. I have also come to enjoy cooking at rather “interesting” hours as my family would say - but discovering new recipes is a key interest of mine. Being able to engage my taste buds with savoury delicacies obtained from recipes all over the world, is just the added cherry on top.

  6. Spending time with family

    7. I believe I would not be the person I am today if it were not for my family. They've helped mold me for the better and continue to do so as I get older. I regard them as my first soulmates as I believe our souls were destined to come together and journey through life together. This is quite the Shakesperian way of saying, "I enjoy spending time with my family".
